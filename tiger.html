Why tigers cannot be domestic animals

  • They are carnivorous
  • They kill others for prey
  • They outgrow humans and become less guided

Top 3 most famous tiger breeds

  • Amur
  • Indian/Bengal

Basic info

Tigers are wild animals and live in dense forests. They have 4 legs and a long tail. They have Orange skins mostly with teeth and paws very strong.
